# Wireframe Sketch

Use this skill before committing to visual styling.

## Rules

- Show structure, hierarchy, flows, and alternatives rather than polished branding.
- Use annotations to explain layout tradeoffs, assumptions, and missing inputs.
- Keep labels realistic enough that the product concept is testable.
- Avoid pretending the wireframe is final UI.
